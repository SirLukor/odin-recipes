Lemon Thyme Chicken Tender

Description

A wonderful tasting choice to prepare your chicken. Making it this way makes it literally impossible for your chicken to come out dry.

The perfect dish for Spring or Summer. Do you choose to make it suring Fall or Winter however, is that no problem. Subsitute the Lemon for an Orange or a Tangerine and the Thyme for a few sprigs of Rosemary and you'll be in heaven in no time again.
